What Are Essential Oils?

Have you ever taken a peppermint leaf and rubbed it in between your fingers? The beautiful aroma that you smell comes from the essential oils found on the plant. Essential oils are the volatile aromatic compounds found in the leaves, stem, bark or flowers of a plant. Essential oils not only play an important role in protecting the plant, they have also play a role in supporting healthy body systems.

Why Quality Matters.

Have you ever grown a garden? Where a plant is grown and the conditions surrounding the growing and harvesting of the plant, plays an important role in the quality of the end product.

The same holds true with essential oils. Plants that are grown and harvested in the proper regions and at the right time will produce a higher quality of essential oil.
